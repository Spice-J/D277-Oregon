Our beautiful Capital City

Salem is the cappital of the state of Oregon. Located in the center of the territory, Salem lies 47 miles south of Portland and an hour from the Cascade Mountains and the ocean beaches.

The city has been experiencing steady growth in recent years and attracts newcomers and tourists to the capital building, which displays art by artists who call the state home.
