Keep Portland Weird!

Portland is Oregon's largest city and sits on the Columbia and Willamette Rivers with Mt.Hood watching over on the east side of the city. Full of parks numerous bridges and microbrew beer as the staple beverage, there is so much to see, do and eat here.

Portland has some great spots to visit. Catch up on your reading at Powell's books, take a drive down the River Gorge and camp out in Mt. Hood National Forest. There's something for everyone here!
